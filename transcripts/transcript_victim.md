Agent: When you think about today's biggest challenge or win, who comes to mind?

       But try this: is there anyone your day affected? Even indirectly?
       Noticing that connection is the beginning of perspective.

       entitlement mindset. And your attention spanned from self-centric concerns.
       That's the shape of your day.
